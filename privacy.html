Privacy Policy – Flash Bargain Drops

Last Updated: 10 Oct 2025

1. Data We Access

  • Your TikTok user ID and username (only for the account owner who authorises our app).

2. How We Use the Data

  • To upload promotional videos to the authorised TikTok account.

OAuth tokens are stored securely on Make.com servers and are deleted automatically when you revoke access. We store no additional personal data.

Our content targets general audiences and is not directed toward children under 13.
